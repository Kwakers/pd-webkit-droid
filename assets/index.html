Help

PdWebKit lets you play your Pure Data patches on Android devices with custom interfaces built in HTML and Javascript. See [the documentation](http://mccormick.cx/projects/PdWebKit/) for instructions on how to build an interface for your patch and get it running on your device.

PdWebKit is copyright Chris McCormick, 2009, 2010.

This program is GPLv3 licensed. A copy of the LICENSE.txt is available online here.

Obtain the sourcecode with bzr:
bzr co http://mccormick.cx/dev/PdWebKit/
